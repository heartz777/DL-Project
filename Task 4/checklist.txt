Checklist: Responsible Social Media Use
✅ Do’s
Think before posting; consider long-term impact.
Verify information before sharing.
Maintain privacy settings and protect personal data.
Communicate respectfully and professionally.
Separate personal and professional profiles when appropriate.
Credit original creators when sharing content.
❌ Don’ts
Do not share confidential or sensitive information.
Do not post offensive, discriminatory, or harmful content.
Do not engage in online arguments or harassment.
Do not overshare personal details (location, financial data, etc.).
Do not spread unverified news or rumors.
Do not post inappropriate content that could affect academic or career opportunities.
