Stay Safe Online: 8-Point Checklist
Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, passwords, or CVV numbers with anyone.
Verify unknown callers claiming to be from banks or government agencies.
Avoid clicking suspicious links received via SMS, email, or messaging apps.
Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
Regularly monitor bank statements and transaction alerts.
Install apps only from official app stores and keep devices updated.
Immediately report financial fraud to the cybercrime helpline 1930.
File complaints through the National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in
).
